Surge Vessel

A surge vessel is a pressure vessel designed to absorb sudden pressure surges and fluctuations in piping systems, preventing water hammer and protecting equipment from damage. It acts as a buffer tank to maintain stable system pressure during pump start-ups, shut-downs, and valve operations.

Our surge vessels are manufactured as per ASME standards with high-quality carbon steel or stainless steel construction. They feature pre-charged nitrogen cushions or bladder systems to effectively dampen pressure transients, ensuring long-lasting performance in water distribution networks, pumping stations, and industrial fluid systems.

01 Quality Assurance & Compliance

Rigorous quality control including material certification, weld mapping, radiography (RT), ultrasonic testing (UT), and hydrostatic pressure testing. Full documentation package with ASME data reports and National Board registration available for all U Stamp vessels.

02 ASME U Stamp Certification

Complete compliance with ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code Section VIII Division 1. Our U Stamp certification guarantees that every pressure vessel meets strict international safety and quality standards, including material traceability, qualified welding procedures, and third-party inspection.

03 R Stamp Repair & Alteration

Authorized R Stamp facility for repair, alteration, and maintenance of ASME certified pressure vessels. Our team performs code-compliant welding, non-destructive testing (NDT), hydrostatic testing, and complete documentation to restore equipment to original code requirements.
